About Us

The ETH Domain Data Stewardship Network (ETH·DSN) aims to establish a data stewardship community within the ETH Domain (EPFL, ETH Zurich, Eawag, Empa, WSL, PSI).
The network’s key goals include:

  • Facilitating the onboarding of new data stewards.
  • Providing a centralized access point for information and resources for data stewards.
  • Acting as a central hub for events organized by ETH·DSN or in partnership with organizations like the Research Software Engineering Community.
These efforts support networking opportunities for the broader ETH Domain community.
